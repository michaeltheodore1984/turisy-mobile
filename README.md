# Turisy

Turisy is a platform that connects travelers with local guides to discover authentic, personalized experiences.

The platform helps visitors explore destinations through the perspective of people who know them best, enabling local guides to share recommendations, experiences, and unique insights beyond traditional tourism.

## Overview

Turisy aims to bridge the gap between travelers seeking meaningful experiences and locals who want to share their knowledge of their communities.

## Features

* Connect travelers with local guides
* Discover personalized recommendations and experiences
* Enable locals to share their expertise and knowledge
* Create more authentic travel experiences
